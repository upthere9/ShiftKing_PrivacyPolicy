# Privacy Policy

**9&U Inc.** ("Company") values the privacy of users of the ShiftKing application ("App") and is committed to protecting personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

This Privacy Policy applies to the ShiftKing mobile application and its related services.

---

## 1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The Company processes personal information for the following purposes:

- Providing and maintaining the App's features and services
- Analyzing errors and improving service quality
- Monitoring and improving app performance
- Delivering advertisements and measuring advertising performance
- Responding to customer inquiries and providing support
- Reviewing work schedules voluntarily submitted by users to improve the default shift schedule database

The Company does not use personal information for any purpose other than those described above.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do Not Directly Collect

ShiftKing does not require user registration or login.

The Company does not directly collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or email address through the App.

### 2.2 Information You Voluntarily Provide

The following information is transmitted to the Company only when you choose to provide it:

- Information included in support emails
- Files attached to support emails
- Shift schedules voluntarily submitted through the schedule submission feature

Submitted schedules are reviewed by the developer solely for the purpose of improving the default shift schedule database.

### 2.3 Information Automatically Collected

The following information may be collected automatically to operate and improve the App:

- Device model
- iOS version
- App version
- App usage information
- Crash and diagnostic information
- Performance information
- Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and advertising-related information, where applicable and subject to your consent when required

This information may be processed through Google Firebase, Google AdMob, and advertising mediation services.

## 5. Permissions Used by the App

ShiftKing may request the following system permissions.

### Calendar

Calendar read and write access is used to allow users to add and manage work schedules in their calendar.

### Notifications

Notification permission is used to provide local reminders for work schedules and other app features.

### iCloud

If enabled by the user, iCloud may be used to back up and restore app data.

The Company does not use these permissions for purposes other than those described above.

---

## 6. Shift Schedule Submission

The App includes an optional shift schedule submission feature.

Schedules are transmitted to the Company only when the user explicitly chooses to submit them.

Submitted schedules are reviewed solely for the purpose of improving the default shift schedule database.

Personal schedules that are not voluntarily submitted remain stored only on the user's device and are not transmitted to the Company's servers.

---

## 7. Retention of Personal Information

The Company retains personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law.

Information provided through customer support emails is retained only for the period necessary to resolve the inquiry.

Shift schedules voluntarily submitted by users may be retained for the period necessary to review and improve the default shift schedule database.

The Company takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including:

- Restricting access to personal information
- Applying encryption where appropriate
- Using up-to-date security technologies
- Limiting and managing personnel with access to personal information
